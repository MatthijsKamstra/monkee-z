Monkee Loader

A library for loading templates and files.

  • No loading animation
  • Files will be parsed as-is
  • No xss protection
  • Whole html will be parsed (make sure your templates have no html/body/head tags)
  • Lightweight
  • usefull for loading files/templates into the page
